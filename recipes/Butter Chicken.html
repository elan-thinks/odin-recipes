🍛 Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

Descriptions

A velvety, spiced tomato-cream sauce hugs every tender piece of chicken.
This Indian classic brings restaurant flavors to your home kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken thighs
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 3 tbsp butter 🧈
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • v1 cup tomato purée 🍅
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Directions:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices. Let sit 1 hr
  2. Sauté onion, garlic, ginger in butter
  3. Add tomato purée and simmer
  4. Add chicken and cook till tender
  5. SPour in cream and simmer. Serve with rice or naan🙌
