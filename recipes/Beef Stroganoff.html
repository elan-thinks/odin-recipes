🥘 Beef Stroganoff

Descriptions

rich and elegant dish with a garlic-kissed cream sauce,
sun-dried tomatoes, and spinach. Perfect for cozy nights or a fancy dinner at home.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef strips
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cups mushrooms 🍄
  • 1 cup heavy cream 🥛
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • Salt & pepper

Directions:

  1. Mear beef, then remove
  2. Sauté onion and mushroom
  3. Stir in flour, broth, and mustard
  4. Return beef and simmer
  5. Stir in sour cream. Serve over egg noodles 🐮🍝
