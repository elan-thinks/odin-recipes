🍝 Creamy Garlic Tuscan Chicken

Descriptions

A rich and elegant dish with a garlic-kissed cream sauce, sun-dried tomatoes, and spinach.
Perfect for cozy nights or a fancy dinner at home.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced 🧄
  • 1 cup heavy cream 🥛
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup baby spinach 🌿
  • ½ cup sun-dried tomatoes 🍅
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese 🧀
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Season chicken and sear in olive oil until golden. Remove.
  2. In the same pan, sauté garlic, then add cream and broth.
  3. Stir in spinach, tomatoes, and cheese. Let it simmer.
  4. Return chicken and cook until done.
  5. Serve with pasta or mashed potatoes. 💛
